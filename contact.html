Frequently Asked Questions

Do your apps collect any data?

No, our apps are designed to work entirely offline and store all data locally on your device. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information.

How do I backup my data?

Since all data is stored locally, you can backup your data through your device's standard backup methods (iCloud for iOS, Google Drive for Android).

Can I transfer my data to a new device?

Yes, you can transfer your data using your device's standard transfer methods or restore from a backup.

Are your apps safe for children?

Absolutely! Our apps are designed with privacy in mind and are safe for users of all ages since no data is collected or transmitted.

How often do you update your apps?

We regularly update our apps to improve functionality and fix any issues. Updates are distributed through the app stores.
