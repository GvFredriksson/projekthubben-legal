Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 2025

Important Notice

This privacy policy applies ONLY to our mobile applications listed on this site. Our mobile applications are designed with privacy in mind - all data is stored locally on your device and is never transmitted to our servers or any third parties. We do not collect, store, or process any personal information.

1. Information We Do NOT Collect

Our applications are designed to operate entirely offline and locally. We do not collect, store, or process any of the following:

  • Personal information (name, email, phone number)
  • Usage data or analytics
  • Device information
  • Location data
  • Any data that you create or input into the applications

2. Local Data Storage

All data created and used within our applications is stored exclusively on your device using:

  • Local device storage (iOS: Core Data, Android: SQLite)
  • Device preferences and settings
  • User-generated content (notes, calculations, etc.)

This data remains under your complete control and is never transmitted to external servers.

3. Third-Party Services

Our applications do not integrate with any third-party services that would collect or transmit your data. We do not use:

  • Analytics services
  • Crash reporting services
  • Advertising networks
  • Social media integrations
  • Cloud storage services

4. App Store Requirements

While our applications do not collect any data, app stores may require privacy policy disclosure. This policy serves to:

  • Comply with app store requirements
  • Provide transparency about our privacy practices
  • Inform users about local-only data storage

5. Data Security

Since all data remains on your device:

  • Your data is protected by your device's security measures
  • We cannot access your data even if we wanted to
  • Data is only accessible to you and anyone with access to your device
  • We recommend using device security features (passcodes, biometrics)

6. Data Backup and Transfer

If you choose to backup or transfer your data:

  • iOS: Data may be included in iCloud backups (controlled by your iCloud settings)
  • Android: Data may be included in Google Drive backups (controlled by your backup settings)
  • These backups are controlled by your device settings, not our applications

7. Children's Privacy

Our applications are safe for users of all ages. Since we do not collect any data, there are no privacy concerns for children using our applications.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. Since our applications do not collect data, changes to this policy will not affect your privacy.

9. Contact Information

Questions About Privacy?

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or our privacy practices, please contact us:

Email: privacy@projekthubben.com

Website: projekthubben.github.io

10. Your Rights

Since we do not collect any personal data, you have complete control over your information. You can:

  • Delete the application and all associated data at any time
  • Control data backup through your device settings
  • Transfer data to other devices using standard device transfer methods
  • Modify or delete any data you create within the applications

This privacy policy is effective as of the date listed above and applies ONLY to mobile applications developed by Projekthubben AB that operate with local-only data storage. This policy does not apply to our web-based systems or other services.
